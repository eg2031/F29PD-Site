TracFit

A health tracking system

  • TracFit is an innovative, intuitive and interactive health monitoring system, aiming to facilitate people keeping on top of all of the data that goes into keeping them fit and happy.
  • With a powerful data focussed dashboard TracFit allows you to maintain control, we manage all of the data process it into an understandable, usable format.
  • Our goal is to diminish the difficulties that come with tracking a multitude of complex metrics, meaning you don't have to get bogged down crunching numbers, you want to get fit, not get smart!
